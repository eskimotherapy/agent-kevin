Kuala Selangor · Saturday

Eagles, hills & a river of stars

🚗 1 h 50 · ~100 kmFirefly night

An easy morning drive out of the city, an afternoon with the silver-leaf monkeys on Bukit Melawati, and the firefly boats on the river after Maghrib.

☀️
Morning
🦅
Bukit Melawati & the silver-leaf monkeys
Lighthouse, old fort walls, and the famously gentle silvered langurs. Take the tram up so little legs are saved for the evening. The long-tailed macaques are the cheeky ones — keep snacks zipped away.
~RM 25 familyStroller-OK via tram
Tram
RM 5/4 adult/child

Before 10am
Tip: buy a bundle of long beans at the foot of the hill — the langurs eat from your hand and the kids will talk about it for weeks.

Afternoon
🍜
Lunch & Dhuhr in town, then check in
Restoran Auntie Kopitiam (Muslim-owned) for mee hoon and seafood fried rice, Dhuhr at the masjid two streets over, then check in at De Palma (family rooms, surau on site, kids' pool) and rest before dark.

Evening
✨
Kampung Kuantan firefly boats
A wooden sampan paddled silently along the berembang trees flickering with synchronized fireflies. Pray Maghrib first; keep voices low and phones dim.
RM 60 / boat (4 pax)Queue after 20:00 weekends
Plan B: if it rains, the Firefly Park Resort jetty at Bukit Belimbing runs covered boats and refunds on cancellation.

Taman Alam Kuala Selangor (nature park)
Mangrove boardwalks and silvered langurs in the wild — a gentle morning alternative if the hill is crowded.
🦗
Sky Mirror (tidal sandbar)
A mirror-flat sandbar that appears at low tide for a boat trip — only on the right tide and date, so check before counting on it.

Good to know

The practical layer — halal, prayers, timing, and what to pack for a riverbank night.

🕌 Halal eating

Vetted: Auntie Kopitiam (KS town), Nelayan Bagan (Sekinchan, verify on arrival). Many Sekinchan seafood houses are non-halal — ask before ordering anywhere new.

🧎 Prayer stops

Masjid India Muslim in KS town; De Palma has a surau; Sungai Besar R&R surau on the drive home. Pack travel mats.

🧳 Pack for the river

Mosquito repellent (fireflies = riverbank), swimwear for the pool, cash (boats and stalls are cash-only), hats for the paddy walk.

🩺 Emergencies

Hospital Tengku Ampuan Jemaah, Sabak Bernam (15 min from Sekinchan). National emergency: 999.
